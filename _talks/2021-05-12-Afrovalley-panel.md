AfroValley is a digital movement and platform that connects farmers, investors and traders of coffee, tea and fruits by way of trustless transactions using blockchain and smart contracts. AfroValley works to remove the middle man from taking disparate profits from growers. The founder organized a panel of blockchain experts in various capacities to discuss the present and future of digital currency and research.
