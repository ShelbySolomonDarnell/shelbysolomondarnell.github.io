excerpt: "This concept paper introduces a digital dashboard that utilizes biometric identification. The system is designed to have any driver's personal settings stored using their biometric identity and have the car load personalized settings once it recognizes the authorized biometric signature. The paper evaluates the biometric solutions to be applied in an automotive setup and chooses the well-proven and most ubiquitous, fingerprint. Personalized settings are stored on a secure server over the network and can only be retrieved and unencrypted using the driver's personal biometric signature."
